Poses for Back Pain

Nov 2025

Support the spine with gentle, controlled movements and mindful breathing. Keep alignment and avoid pain.

  • Standing knee lifts: brace core and lift smoothly
  • Side lunges: hinge hips back, knees tracking
  • Gentle spinal rolls: slow segmental articulation

Do not force range, avoid jerky motions, and stop if symptoms worsen.
