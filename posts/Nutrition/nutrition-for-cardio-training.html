Nutrition for Cardio Training

Nov 2025

Fuel sessions with balanced meals and proper hydration. Focus on easily digested carbs pre-workout and a mix of protein and carbs post-workout.

  • Hydration: sip water regularly
  • Pre-workout: light snack 60–90 minutes before
  • Post-workout: protein 20–30g with carbs

Adjust portions to your goals and consult nutrition guidance for specific conditions.
