Teacher Presentation

Design rationale, competitive analysis, mood board, and prototype overview for the redesign.

Competitive Analysis

We reviewed three comparable sites focusing on hierarchy, accessibility, and performance.

  • Academic health portal with strong AA color contrast and structured navigation.
  • Wellness education platform using a clear Z-pattern and modular components.
  • Course marketplace optimized for mobile-first interactions and 12-column grids.

Design Decisions

  • Established a six-color palette with AA contrast for key interactions.
  • Applied an 8px spacing system and a 24px baseline for flow.
  • Selected Inter for headings and Manrope for body to balance legibility and tone.
  • Implemented 12-column grid layouts consistent with Bootstrap across breakpoints.
  • Defined component standards for buttons, forms, and cards with 44px touch targets.
  • Enhanced hero sections with layered imagery and clear CTAs.

Wireframes

Mobile-first frames demonstrate F-pattern and Z-pattern flow using a 12-column grid.

Accessibility and Performance

  • WCAG AA compliant color pairs for text and UI controls.
  • Lazy-loaded imagery and responsive `srcset` for performance under 2 seconds.
  • Keyboard-focus states for interactive elements to ensure operability.
